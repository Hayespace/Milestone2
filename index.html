Introducing Music Machine, a playful virtual instrument and music recording website that combines the convenience of a digital piano, two classic drum machines, and an audio recorder with downloadable sample libraries. With its user-friendly interface featuring five collapsible panels, Music Machine offers a versatile platform for musicians to explore their creativity.

The digital piano on Music Machine captures the essence of a real acoustic piano, providing a rich and authentic sound experience. Users can play the piano using their computer keyboard, with specific keys assigned for each note. By simply pressing the A, W, S, E, D, F, T, G, Y, H, U, J, K, O, L, P, and : keys, musicians can produce beautiful melodies and harmonies.

While currently offering a single sound library, Music Machine provides a diverse collection of high-quality samples that showcase vintage drum kits. With the Roland 808 and MPC drum machines, users can explore iconic drum sounds that have shaped the music industry. Each drum machine comes with its own unique set of samples, allowing musicians to experiment and create beats that resonate with their artistic vision.

The audio recorder feature on Music Machine enables musicians to record their compositions with ease. With the capability to record a single track at a time, users can lay down their melodies, harmonies, or vocals effortlessly.
